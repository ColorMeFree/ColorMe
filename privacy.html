Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Our Commitment to Privacy

ColorMeFree is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe experience for families. Our service is designed for adults (18+) to create custom coloring books for children.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Important: We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

  • Our website is designed for and directed to adults (18+)
  • We do not collect children's names, ages, photos, or personal information
  • All interactions are with the parent/guardian purchaser
  • Order communications are sent only to the parent's email address
  • Books are referenced by order number, not child's name

Information We Collect

From Adult Purchasers:

  • Email address (for order updates and delivery)
  • Shipping address (for book delivery)
  • Payment information (processed securely by Shopify)
  • Book creation prompts (to generate coloring pages)

How We Use Information

  • Process and fulfill your coloring book orders
  • Generate custom coloring pages based on your prompts
  • Send order confirmations and shipping updates
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our service (using anonymized data)

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Encrypted payment processing
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Regular security assessments

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services:

  • Shopify: Secure payment processing and order management
  • Lulu.com: Print-on-demand book production and shipping
  • Plausible Analytics: Privacy-friendly website analytics (no cookies)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Contact us with privacy concerns
